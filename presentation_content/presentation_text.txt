SLIDE 2: Business & Safety Context
Duration: 9 seconds
================================================================================

Street monitoring systems capture traffic, pedestrians, and vehicles.

CCTV cameras are already there:
        • 24h feeds generate huge volumes of video
        • Most frames are routine, a few contain risky behaviours
        • We want traffic violations and unsafe patterns surfaced quickly


================================================================================
SLIDE 3: The Scale Problem: 24h Video
Duration: 10 seconds
================================================================================

A single 24-hour camera feed at 0.1 FPS yields thousands of frames.

Naive manual workflow:
        • Export all frames
        • Manually draw boxes on every candidate object
        • Repeat for every camera, every day

Result: annotation spirals out of control as we add more cameras.


================================================================================
SLIDE 4: What We Actually Want
Duration: 9 seconds
================================================================================

Safety teams do not want raw frames – they want structured signals:
        • Clips where people and vehicles interact in risky ways
        • Evidence for near-misses and policy violations
        • Clear, auditable datasets to fine-tune detection models

Our job: turn always-on video into a targeted, annotator-friendly dataset.


================================================================================
SLIDE 5: Pipeline Overview – 4 Stages
Duration: 9 seconds
================================================================================

Stage 1 – Preprocessing: video → cleaned frames
Stage 2 – Pre-tagging: frames → COCO detections
Stage 3 – Cleanup: raw pre-tags → filtered COCO
Stage 4 – Sampling & Report: curated clips + MD report

Each stage is config-driven, logged, and can be tuned per client site.
